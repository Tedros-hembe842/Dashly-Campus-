How Dashly works

1. You order from Jolly

Pick your favorite Jolly Pizza items and tell us your dorm & time window. Orders are limited while we pilot.

2. A runner picks it up

Dashly runners are CWRU students assigned by “backyard” — North, Middle, and South — matching each Jolly location for fast, predictable pickup.

3. We meet you at your dorm

We text you when we’re on the way and meet you at your dorm entrance.

If you don’t meet us within the first 5 minutes, we’ll bring your order back to Jolly Pizza, and you’ll need to pick it up from the restaurant.

This pilot is early — thank you for being patient as we build the foundation. Future phases include warming stations and secure food lockers.

For students

Skip the cold walk across campus. Dashly brings Jolly Pizza to your dorm entryway during select night windows.

We’re launching small, backyard by backyard, to make delivery actually work for how this campus moves.

A simple flat delivery fee and clear windows so you know exactly what you're paying for and when it will arrive.

For restaurants & campus partners

Win each backyard first

Instead of trying to serve the whole campus at once, Dashly grows volume around each Jolly location — North, Middle, and South — where it’s easiest to serve consistently.

No third-party app chaos

Student-run operations, direct communication, and simple reporting instead of high-fee marketplace apps and random drivers who don’t know campus.

Student-built infrastructure

Dedicated runners today; warming equipment, locker concepts, and tighter data later. Dashly is designed as a campus-native delivery layer, not just another account on a delivery app.
