How Dashly works

1. You order from the Jolly in your backyard

Based on where you live, you’ll order from the Jolly location closest to you: Jolly Pizza (North), Jolly Scholar (Middle), or Southside Scholar (South). During the pilot, you’ll only see delivery options for your backyard.

2. A runner from your backyard picks it up

Dashly runners are CWRU students assigned to the same backyard they live in. North, Middle, and South each have their own small crew so pickup and handoff stay predictable and tight.

3. We meet you at your dorm (5-minute rule)

We text you when we’re on the way and meet you at your dorm entrance. If you don’t meet us within the first 5 minutes, we’ll bring your order back to the Jolly location it came from and you’ll need to pick it up there.

This pilot is early — thank you for being patient as we prove the model. Future phases include warming stations and secure food lockers so pickups can be more flexible.

For students

Stay in. Eat Jolly.

Skip the cold walk across campus. When your backyard is active, Dashly brings Jolly to your dorm entryway during select night windows.

Backyard-based delivery

Your options are based on where you live, not some huge delivery radius. That keeps routes short, handoffs faster, and mistakes lower while we pilot.

Simple and transparent

A flat delivery fee and clear time windows — you know which Jolly you’re ordering from, what you’re paying, and roughly when it will arrive.

For restaurants & campus partners

Win each backyard first

Instead of trying to serve the whole campus at once, Dashly grows volume around each Jolly location — North, Middle, and South — where it’s easiest to serve consistently.

No third-party app chaos

Student-run operations, direct communication, and simple reporting instead of high-fee marketplace apps and random drivers who don’t know campus.

Student-built infrastructure

Dedicated runners today; warming equipment, locker concepts, and tighter data later. Dashly is designed as a campus-native delivery layer, not just another account on a delivery app.
