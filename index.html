How Dashly works

1. You order from Jolly

Pick your favorite Jolly Pizza items and tell us your dorm and time window. Orders are limited while we pilot so we can run clean operations.

2. A runner picks it up

Dashly runners are CWRU students assigned by “backyard” (North, Middle, South) for fast, predictable pickup from each Jolly location.

3. We meet you at your dorm

We text you when we’re on the way. If you can’t meet us within 5 minutes after we arrive, we’ll bring your order back to Jolly and you can grab it directly there. While we pilot, we’re keeping it simple — but long-term we’re aiming for warming stations and lockers so your food can stay hot even if you’re delayed.

For students

Built around dorm life

Skip the cold walk across campus. Dashly brings Jolly Pizza to your dorm entryway during select night windows in your backyard.

Backyard-based delivery

We’re starting small, block by block, with separate crews for North, Middle, and South — so your runner actually knows your side of campus.

Simple, flat pricing

A flat delivery fee and limited windows while we pilot so we can stay reliable and learn what works before expanding.

For restaurants & campus partners

Win each backyard first

Instead of trying to serve the whole campus at once, Dashly grows volume around each Jolly location — North, Middle, and South — where it’s easiest to serve consistently.

No third-party app chaos

Student-run operations, direct communication, and simple reporting instead of high-fee marketplace apps and random drivers who don’t know campus.

Student-built infrastructure

Dedicated runners today; warming equipment and future locker concepts later. Dashly is designed as a campus-native delivery layer, not just another tab in a delivery app.
