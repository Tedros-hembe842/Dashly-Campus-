How Dashly works

1. You order from Jolly

Pick your favorite Jolly Pizza items and tell us your dorm & preferred time window. Orders are limited while we pilot.

2. A runner picks it up

Dashly runners are CWRU students assigned by “backyard” (North, Middle, South), so pickup is fast, predictable, and built around each Jolly location.

3. We meet you at your dorm

We text you when we’re on the way. If you can’t meet us within 5 minutes, your order goes back to the Jolly location. During the pilot, we’re learning what works now while we work toward warming stations and future food lockers.

How blocks work

• Students get a text: “North side block now open.” • Students order in that window. • Runners pick up multiple orders at once. • Faster + predictable for everyone.

For restaurants & campus partners

Win each backyard first

Instead of trying to serve the whole campus at once, Dashly grows volume around North, Middle, and South — where it's easiest to serve consistently.

No marketplace chaos

Student-run communication, predictable timing, and simple reporting — not random gig-economy drivers.

Student-built infrastructure

Runners today. Warming equipment later. Lockers later. Dashly is a campus-native delivery layer.
