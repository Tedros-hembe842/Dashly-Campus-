How Dashly works

1. You order from Jolly

Pick your favorite Jolly Pizza items and tell us your dorm & preferred time window. Orders are limited while we pilot.

2. A runner picks it up

Dashly runners are CWRU students assigned by “backyard” (North, Middle, South) so pickup is fast, predictable, and built around each Jolly location.

3. We meet you at your dorm

We text you when we’re on the way and meet you at your dorm entryway. If you can’t meet us within 5 minutes, your order goes back to the Jolly location for pickup. During the pilot, we’re learning what works now while we work toward warming stations and future food lockers.

For students

We’re starting small and learning block by block. At first, Dashly is open to select dorms in each “backyard.” Greek housing will be added later once we prove demand and operations.

North Backyard – Jolly Pizza

All first-year housing, STJ, and The Village. North is our most concentrated late-night demand, so this backyard helps us prove the model quickly.

Greek housing on North is excluded for the initial pilot.

Middle Backyard – Jolly Scholar

Hessler housing, apartments above Plum, Twin Gables, and the 1716 building. As we see traction, Jolly Scholar delivery opens up to these central spots.

Built around short walking routes and predictable pickup from the main Jolly Scholar location.

South Backyard – Southside Scholar

Southside dorms at the bottom and top of the hill. This backyard focuses on bringing late-night food down the hill so students don’t have to make the full walk back up.

Greek housing on South is also excluded in the earliest pilot phase.

As we learn what works, we’ll gradually add more dorms, windows, and eventually Greek housing where it makes sense.

Delivery windows (pilot)

We’re not a 24/7 app. Dashly is built around clear delivery blocks so runners can batch orders, move safely, and keep fees fair.

Block-based delivery

Instead of random on-demand runs, we open specific windows (for example, late-night blocks on select nights). You’ll get a message like: “Jolly Pizza bulk delivery to North is open—get your order in for this block.”

Clear expectations

Each block comes with a promised arrival range (e.g. within a 45–60 minute window). You’ll know which backyard is active and when we’re headed your way.

Still being refined

Exact days and hours will be finalized with Jolly’s team. Early pilot interest will help us decide which nights and backyards go live first.

For restaurants & campus partners

Win each backyard first

Instead of trying to serve the whole campus at once, Dashly grows volume around each Jolly location—North, Middle, and South—where it’s easiest to serve consistently.

No third-party app chaos

Student-run operations, direct communication, and simple reporting instead of high-fee marketplace apps and random drivers who don’t know campus.

Student-built infrastructure

Dedicated runners today; warming equipment, locker concepts, and tighter data later. Dashly is designed as a campus-native delivery layer, not just another account on a delivery app.
