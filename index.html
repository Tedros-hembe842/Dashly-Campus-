How Dashly works

1. You order from Jolly

Pick your favorite Jolly Pizza items and tell us your dorm & time window. Orders are limited while we pilot.

2. A runner picks it up

Dashly runners are CWRU students assigned by “backyard” (North, Middle, South) for fast pickup.

3. We meet you at your dorm

We text you when we're on the way. If you can’t meet us, we’ll use a warming station or secure locker.
